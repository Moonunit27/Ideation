Sequence

Sequence is boardgame typically played by players of teams (of even numbers). Players are given 7 random cards and they must use the cards in their hand to create a row of 5 stones as well as try to stop the other player from doing the same. players may only place stones on spaces if they hold that card in theor hand. after they play their card it is put into the discard pile and they draw a new card. In sequence you will notice that there are no spaces for jacks on the board. This is because 2 eyed jacks are wild and 1 eyed jacks may remove one of the other players stones from the board.
